About the Founder

The Man Behind
the Lens

In a world rushing past, Yog Rahangdale slows time. Born with a camera in his hands and poetry in his heart, Yog has spent years perfecting the art of capturing the invisible — the trembling breath before the vows, the grandmother's tearful smile, the first look that says everything words cannot.

"Every frame I capture is a love letter — to the moment, to the people in it, and to the memories that deserve to live forever."

Based in Maharashtra, Yog Creative Photography has become synonymous with cinematic grandeur and emotional depth. From sweeping drone vistas to intimate candid portraits, every project is approached as a feature film — your story, told with breathtaking visual poetry.

Trained in cinematic color grading, aerial cinematography, and storytelling composition, Yog brings Hollywood-level production quality to every wedding, every pre-shoot, every milestone worth remembering.
